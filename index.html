My top 3 favorite things about UPMC Children’s Hospital

by Patrick

Posted on October 5, 2018 at 12:34 PM

My top 3 things

1)How nice and caring all of the hospital staff is. I didn’t think it was possible to feel this at home here!

2) All of the amazing events and resources the Child Life Department connects us with. I loved the Harry Potter movie event last week, except Voldemort is scary and I don’t like him. His nose is weird!

3)The ability to connect with my friends on this platform! I really miss my friends at school since my treatment lasts 6 months, and it’s so nice to have made new friends here.

Thank you for this meaningful post!

UPMC Doctor, UPMC
