Designed With You In Mind.

Building Buddies is a platform which focuses on making your long-term hospital stay the best it can be! We aim to provide a platform that allows you to build relationships with others in similar situations, and share alike interests.

Connect with Friends And Communities

Connect with new friends within your hospital who share similar interests and hobbies as you. You can also join interest and support groups with people from all over the world!

Discover New Opportunities For Fun

Be up to date about new events in the hospital that align with your interests. Additionally, you can play mini-games and compete with your friends!

Together in the Hospital Journey

Our goal is to create a home away from home for you by providing resources to build fun, meaningful, and long-lasting relationships.
